Gulab Jamun Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Milk Powder
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Ghee or Unsalted Butter
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom Pods
  • Rose Water
  • Saffron Strands
  • Pistachios (optional, for garnish)

Quantity:

  • 1 cup Milk Powder
  • 1/4 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/4 tsp Baking Powder
  • 2 tbsp Ghee or Unsalted Butter
  • 1/4 cup Milk (approx)
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1 cup Water
  • 4-5 Cardamom Pods
  • 1 tsp Rose Water
  • A few Saffron Strands
  • Pistachios (optional, for garnish)

Tips:

  • Ensure the oil is not too hot when frying Gulab Jamuns to avoid burning.
  • Knead the dough gently, do not over-knead, or else Gulab Jamuns may become hard.
  • Soak the fried Gulab Jamuns in warm sugar syrup for at least 1 hour for best results.
  • Garnish with chopped pistachios for added flavor and presentation.

Recipe:

  1. In a bowl, mix milk powder, all-purpose flour, and baking powder.
  2. Add ghee or unsalted butter and mix well until it resembles breadcrumbs.
  3. Gradually add milk and knead into a smooth dough. Cover and let it rest for 10-15 minutes.
  4. In a saucepan, combine sugar, water, cardamom pods, rose water, and saffron. Bring to a boil and simmer for 5-7 minutes to make sugar syrup.
  5. Divide the dough into small portions and shape them into smooth balls without any cracks.
  6. Heat oil or ghee in a pan on low-medium heat. Fry the Gulab Jamuns until golden brown.
  7. Drain and immediately soak them in warm sugar syrup for at least 1 hour.
  8. Garnish with chopped pistachios and serve warm or at room temperature.
